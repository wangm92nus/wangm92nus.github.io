Biography

I am a Senior Research Fellow at National University of Singapore, Singapore. I received my Ph.D. from Soochow University in 2021. Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Harvard Medical School, Harvard University, USA, and a Research Scientist at the Institute of High Performance Computing (IHPC), Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR), Singapore.

My research interests:

  • Medical Image Analysis, ⚡Artificial Intelligence, ⚡Computer Vision, ⚡Deep Learning.
